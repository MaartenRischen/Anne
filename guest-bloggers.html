Guest Bloggers

Guidelines for contributing to our blog

We Welcome Guest Posts

Anne R. Allen's Blog... with Ruth Harris accepts a limited number of guest posts each year from writers, publishing professionals, and industry experts. Our readers are writers at every stage of their careers, from beginners to seasoned professionals. We look for posts that offer practical, actionable advice with a warm, approachable tone.

Topic Focus

Fresh perspectives on writing craft or current publishing industry issues

Content Style

Informational rather than promotional with light-hearted tone

Word Count

1000–1500 words with subheaders approximately every 300 words

Pitch Email

Send one or two sentences about your proposed subject to annerallen.allen@gmail.com

Deadline

One week before publication

Availability

Must reply to comments on publication day

Booking

Approximately three months in advance

Spots

12–15 per year

No AI Content

All content must be human-written

Compensation

Unpaid, but blog will spotlight your book or service

Notable Past Contributors

Over the years, this blog has been honored to feature guest posts from an extraordinary range of voices in the writing and entertainment world. Our contributors have included Golden Globe winners, Emmy nominees, bestselling authors, literary agents, and publishing industry experts. Their insights have helped make this blog a trusted resource for writers at every stage of their careers.

About This Blog

Anne R. Allen and Ruth Harris have been helping writers navigate the shifting publishing industry since 2009. From craft tips to scam alerts, we cover everything authors need to thrive.
